Pre-release notes from your's truly

  • The game is not finished
  • Think about this as Early Access
  • I would probably call this the 0.8 version
  • This took a fair bit longer than what I thought
  • Boy did it take me a long time to get those Community Chests looking good
  • At the moment, you can move pieces arround the board and examine the diferent properties
  • Also if you land in jail there's no way to get out as I havent implemented getting out of jail yet

  • One of my inital ideas was to have the home screen be a settings page where you input player's info, etc.
  • As of now that data is all hard coded into the data.js file

Fun fact: every single graphical value in the program is relative to a single scale factor
As a result, reloading the page on super small or super large resolutions still looks good
